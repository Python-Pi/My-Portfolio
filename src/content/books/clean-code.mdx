## Why this book stuck with me

Working through the examples sharpened my intuition for naming, extraction, and incremental refactoring. The iterative refactorings reinforced the value of relentless clarity in production code.

## Memorable idea

"Leave the campground cleaner than you found it." Ship the fix, but always scout for a tiny clean-up that prevents the same bug from reappearing.
